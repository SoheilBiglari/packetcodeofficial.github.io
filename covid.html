OdiseeRockt 09-10 juli

TO ENTER THE FESTIVAL AREA, YOU MUST COMPLY WITH ONE OF THE FOLLOWING 4 RULES:

1. YOU NEED TO BE IN POSSESSION OF A VACCINATION CERTIFICATE (2 DOSES + 14 DAYS INCUBATION PERIOD OR 1 DOSE JOHNSON & JOHNSON + 14 DAYS INCUBATION PERIOD).
OR
2. YOU CAN SUBMIT PROOF OF A NEGATIVE PCR TEST, VALID FOR DAY OF TEST + 2 DAYS.
OR
3. YOU CAN SUBMIT PROOF OF A NEGATIVE RAPID ANTI- GEN TEST, VALID FOR DAY OF TEST + 1 DAY.
OR
4. YOU NEED TO BE IN POSSESSION OF A RECOVERY CERTIFICATE WITH A VALIDITY PERIOD THAT STARTS 10 DAYS AFTER THE DATE OF THE FIRST POSITIVE PCR TEST AND ENDS 180 DAYS AFTER THE DATE OF THAT FIRST POSITIVE TEST.
